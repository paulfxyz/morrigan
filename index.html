Why Morrigan

The moment that changed everything

Someone close to me died suddenly. No warning, no time to prepare. What followed was months of painful uncertainty — digital accounts nobody could access, passwords lost to the void, messages they had always meant to send but never did.

That experience made something clear: we prepare our physical estates with lawyers and notaries. But our digital lives — the photos, the writings, the financial access, the final words — exist in a lawless limbo, locked behind encryption that dies with us.

I built Morrigan because no company should hold the keys to your last words. No algorithm should decide what survives you. This is personal sovereignty over the one thing no one can take from you: your legacy.

— Paul Fleury, founder

The Stack

Built on truth, not trust

Client-Side Encryption

Your data is encrypted on your device before it ever touches a network. We evaluate AES-256-GCM and XChaCha20-Poly1305 via libsodium — industry-standard, cryptographically audited.

AES-256-GCM · XChaCha20

Shamir's Secret Sharing

Your encryption key is mathematically split into N shares. Any K-of-N combination reconstructs it. No single person — not your beneficiaries, not us — can access your data alone.

K-of-N threshold · No single point of failure

Bring Your Own Storage

Store your encrypted vault on our servers, or connect any S3-compatible endpoint, SFTP server, or WebDAV host. Morrigan never stores unencrypted data. Ever.

S3 · SFTP · WebDAV · Self-hosted

How It Works

Four steps to sovereign peace of mind

Morrigan makes cryptographic security human. The hard parts are handled automatically — you stay in control of the meaning.

01

Create your space

Upload files, write letters, record voice messages, save passwords. Anything you want to preserve — documents, photos, final words. Rich text, audio, any file format.

02

Encrypt everything

Your vault is encrypted client-side with a unique key. You download and safeguard this key file. We never see it. Without it, the data is mathematically irretrievable — by anyone.

03

Set your conditions

Define what gets shared, with whom, and when. Configure a dead man's switch: if you stop responding for 7, 14, or 30 days, access is triggered. You stay in control until the very end.

04

Share with your people

Beneficiaries receive a private link with instructions. Your key is distributed via Shamir's Secret Sharing — no single person has full access. Only together, only when needed, does the vault open.
