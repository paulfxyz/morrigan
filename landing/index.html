Why it exists

Built for the moments
that matter most.

When someone dies, their digital life rarely survives them cleanly. Bank accounts go frozen. Passwords disappear. Messages intended for loved ones never arrive.

Morrígan is the vault that changes that. Build it once — folders for finances, letters, credentials, anything. It waits, silently, until the moment it's needed. Then delivers exactly what you intended, to exactly who you chose.

Secure Vault

Zero-knowledge encryption, built in from the start.

Your vault is encrypted client-side with AES-256-GCM before it ever touches our servers. We never see your passphrase or your data.
What you get

Your entire digital life,
in one encrypted vault.

Passwords, files, messages, accounts — encrypted client-side and delivered only when the time comes.

Encrypted Vault

AES-256-GCM, keys derived with Argon2id, never leaving your browser. Store passwords, files, notes, credentials — anything, in any folder.

Dead Man's Switch

Set a check-in interval — weekly, monthly, yearly. If you stop responding, Morrígan waits through your grace period, then delivers your vault exactly as instructed.

Multi-Beneficiary

Assign different folders to different people. Your partner gets the finances. Your sibling gets the memories. Your lawyer gets the legal documents.

Zero-Knowledge

Your passphrase never leaves your device. We store only ciphertext. Even if our servers were seized, your data would be unreadable. By design, not by policy.

Two-Factor Auth

Phone (SMS) + authenticator app. You must keep at least two active methods enrolled at all times. One compromised factor is never enough to open your vault.

Time-Locked Messages

Write letters, record voice notes, attach files — to be delivered on a specific date. A birthday. An anniversary. Or the day after you're gone.

Common questions.

Is Morrígan really free?
Yes. Creating a vault, adding folders, assigning beneficiaries, and setting a dead man's switch are all completely free. We offer a paid membership for hosted storage and community access, but the core product has no cost.
What happens if Morrígan shuts down?
Because Morrígan is open source and your data is encrypted client-side, you can export your vault at any time and import it into a self-hosted instance. We can't hold your data hostage.
Where does my data live?
Each folder chooses its own storage. You can use Morrígan's hosted storage (1 GB free, up to 50 GB for members), self-host on your own server, or connect a third-party provider like S3 or WebDAV. Folders in the same vault can each use a different backend.
What if I lose my passphrase?
Your passphrase is the only key to your vault. We do not store it, cannot reset it, and have no backdoor. This is by design — zero-knowledge means we are technically incapable of helping. Write it down. Keep it somewhere safe.
Why do I need two-factor authentication?
Morrígan protects documents that may not be opened for years or decades. Your passphrase alone is not enough — you must keep at least two active methods enrolled (phone SMS + authenticator app). If one is compromised, the second keeps your vault sealed.
