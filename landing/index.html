Zero-Knowledge

Your data encrypted client-side. We hold no keys. We see no content. Only you can unlock your vault.

Blockchain Anchor

SHA-256 hashed, Bitcoin-anchored. Immutable proof of existence. No content on-chain — only your hash.

👥

Shamir Shares

M-of-N key splitting. No single point of failure. Distribute trust across people or devices you choose.

⏱

Dead Man's Switch

Missed check-ins trigger delivery chain automatically. Configurable intervals and grace periods.

📱

3-Channel Identity

3-channel identity: email OTP, phone SMS, and TOTP authenticator. At least 2 of 3 must confirm every sensitive vault action. No single channel is ever sufficient alone.

🔮

Post-Quantum Ready

Kyber & Dilithium migration on roadmap. NIST PQC standards. Built to outlast today's cryptography.

Cryptographic Stack

Encryption you can audit.

Every algorithm is open-source, battle-tested, and replaceable.

⚡ PRIMARY

XChaCha20-Poly1305

256-bit symmetric cipher with authentication. 192-bit nonce. Audited in libsodium and widely deployed.

Learn more →
🔑 KDF

Argon2id

Memory-hard key derivation. 64MB RAM per attempt. Defeats GPU attacks and brute-force at scale.

Learn more →
🔀 SPLITTING

Shamir's Secret Sharing

M-of-N threshold scheme. Vault key split among trusted parties. No single piece reveals anything.

Learn more →
⛓ DEFAULT

OpenTimestamps

Bitcoin-anchored proof of existence. Only SHA-256 hash on-chain. Free, open-source, and permanent.
