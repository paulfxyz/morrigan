Authentication · Security

Two factors. No exceptions.

Access to your vault requires at least two active authentication methods at all times. Your passphrase alone is never enough — if one method is lost or compromised, the second keeps your vault sealed.

Phone (SMS) — a one-time code sent to your registered number
Authenticator app — TOTP code from Google Authenticator, Authy, or any RFC 6238 app
Minimum two active — you must maintain at least two enrolled methods or vault access is suspended

Morrígan protects documents that may not be opened for years or decades. Multi-factor authentication is non-negotiable — it is the first line of defence for everything inside your vault.

Blockchain · Immutable Record

Anchor your vault permanently onto the blockchain.

You can record a cryptographic fingerprint of your vault — or any individual folder — onto a public blockchain. Every change you make creates a new on-chain entry: an immutable, timestamped proof that this version of your vault existed at this exact moment in time. No one can alter, backdate, or deny it.

Nothing private is ever written to the chain — only a hash. Your encrypted vault stays on your chosen storage. The blockchain simply acts as a tamper-proof public notary.

Proof of existence — timestamp any folder or your full vault at a point in time
Change log on-chain — every update writes a new hash, building an auditable history
Beneficiary-verifiable — anyone can check the chain to confirm integrity when the vault is delivered
Zero private data on-chain — only SHA-256 hashes are submitted, nothing readable or reversible

Enhanced Cryptography

Layer more cryptographic standards on top of your vault.

Morrígan defaults to AES-256-GCM + Argon2id — already best-in-class. But if you need more, you can tie your vault to additional cryptographic protocols, signing standards, and zero-knowledge proofs. Designed for high-value or institutional use cases where default encryption is not enough.

Add Shamir's Secret Sharing — split your decryption key between multiple trusted holders
Apply post-quantum cryptography — CRYSTALS-Kyber and CRYSTALS-Dilithium for future-proof protection
ZK proof of contents — prove a document exists and belongs to you, without revealing it
Hardware key support — tie your vault to a YubiKey or HSM for institutional-grade access control
