Why we built this

Born from loss.

"

Morrigan is not a product born from a pitch deck. It is an answer to a question I could not stop asking: what happens to the things we meant to say? In 2019, a mentor was killed in Damascus. That same Christmas, my father died. Neither left a way to be found digitally. Everything they had built — inaccessible, ambiguous, gone. This is the tool I needed and could not find. I am building it so nobody else has to face that silence.

PF
Paul Fleury
Founder, Morrigan Foundation — Lisbon 🇵🇹
🎯
No Business Model

There is no paid tier. No premium features. No advertising. No data monetisation. Morrigan is useful or it isn't. The code is the product.

💰
Donation-Funded

Running costs are covered entirely by voluntary donations. Server hosting, domain registration, Bitcoin node, security tooling. Nothing more.

🌐
Transparent Finances

Every donation and expenditure is recorded publicly. We operate with the same transparency we ask of our cryptographic architecture.

What we pay for

What we don't do

We don't pay salaries. We don't buy advertising. We don't hire growth hackers. We don't have investors. Morrigan runs on code and community.
