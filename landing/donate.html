Our Mission

Why non-profit matters.

No Monetization

We will never sell your data, add ads, or create a freemium model that locks critical features. The vault is free. Period.

Audit-First

Every donation above operational costs goes toward independent security audits. Because trust must be earned, not assumed.

Post-Quantum Commitment

Our foundation charter requires us to always adopt the strongest available encryption. Donations fund this migration.

Founder's Note

A personal note.

I built Morrigan after losing the people I loved most. In 2019, my mentor was killed in Damascus. On Christmas of that year, my father died. I started asking a question I couldn't stop: what happens to the things we meant to say? Morrigan is my answer. It is not a startup. It is not a product. It is a foundation — with all the obligations that word implies. Every donation is a vote that this should exist in the world. Thank you for being part of it.
— Paul Fleury, Founder & Lead Developer
