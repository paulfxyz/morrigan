Our Mission

Why non-profit matters.

A for-profit vault is a product. A non-profit vault is a promise. The difference is everything when the subject is your legacy.

🚫

No Monetization

Morrigan will never sell your data, never introduce paywalled features for core functionality, and never serve advertisements. Non-profit status makes these commitments legally binding, not aspirational.

🔍

Audit-First

A portion of every donation is reserved for independent security audits. We believe the strongest claim any security product can make is: "We paid independent experts to try to break this."

⚛️

Post-Quantum Commitment

Our foundation charter legally commits us to migrate to the strongest available encryption standards as they emerge. This includes post-quantum cryptography. No investor vote required — it is already law for us.

Fund Allocation

Where your money goes.

We publish annual financial summaries. Every allocation is traceable, every expenditure justified.

50%
Infrastructure

Servers, storage, CDN, email delivery, SMS verification, and the compute costs associated with blockchain anchoring. The unglamorous foundation everything else runs on.

30%
Security Audits

Independent third-party cryptographic audits by qualified security firms. This is the highest priority non-infrastructure expenditure. A product that has never been audited is not a secure product.

15%
Development

Post-quantum cryptography migration, mobile applications, hardware key support, and the ongoing maintenance of the open-source codebase. Development expenses are capped to prevent mission drift.

5%
Legal & Admin

Foundation registration, legal compliance, data protection obligations (GDPR), and the administrative costs of maintaining a properly-governed non-profit entity.

Founder's Note

A personal note.

I built Morrigan after losing the people I loved most.

In 2019, my mentor was killed in Damascus. On Christmas of that year, my father died. I started asking a question I couldn't stop: what happens to the things we meant to say?

I searched for tools that could answer that question with the seriousness it deserved. I found password managers, cloud drives, and lawyers — none of which understood the problem. None of which were designed to outlast themselves.

Morrigan is my answer. It is not a startup. It is not a product. It is a foundation — with all the obligations that word implies.

I cannot promise that the people you love will understand everything you leave them. But I can promise that Morrigan will deliver it exactly as you intended — sealed, timestamped, and impossible to alter.

If you believe this matters, please support it. Not for me. For the next person who searches for a tool this serious, and finds one.

PF
Paul Fleury
Founder & Lead Developer · Morrigan Foundation
