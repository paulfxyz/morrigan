Non-Profit Foundation

Support the Foundation

Morrigan is a non-profit project. It will always be free. If you believe everyone deserves sovereignty over their digital legacy, please consider supporting our work.

Our Commitment
We are building Morrigan as a public good — open-source, non-profit, and free forever. Every euro donated goes to infrastructure, security audits, and translations. No investor pressure. No paywalls. No compromise.
Financial Transparency

How funds are used

Every donation is allocated to the things that directly serve the mission — not offices, not marketing, not growth teams.

Infrastructure & hosting 40%
Security audits & bug bounties 35%
Translation & localization 15%
Operations & legal 10%

Accountability

Our transparency pledge

Full financial disclosure, every year

Every year, Morrigan Foundation publishes a full financial report: income sources, expenses, salaries, infrastructure costs. Capped salaries (3× local median wage). No surprises. No opacity. You have the right to know exactly how your money is used — and we commit to making that easy.

Annual report Published every January, covering the prior fiscal year
Capped salaries No team member earns more than 3× the local median wage
Real-time ledger Major expenditures tracked and published quarterly
