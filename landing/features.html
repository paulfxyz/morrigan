Core Features

Built for permanence.

Every tool in Morrigan is engineered around a single promise: your vault survives, your privacy holds, and the right people receive the right things — on your terms, not ours.

🔐
Zero-Knowledge Vault

All encryption runs in your browser via libsodium.js. XChaCha20-Poly1305 authenticated encryption. Argon2id key derivation. Zero server knowledge — by design.

⏱
Dead Man's Switch

Set check-in intervals: daily, weekly, monthly. Configurable grace period. Beneficiaries are notified and guided through delivery only after the switch fires.

👥
Beneficiary System

Assign specific vault items to specific people. Granular control. A letter for your partner, credentials for your executor, photos for your children.

🛡
3-Channel Identity

Email OTP, Phone SMS, TOTP authenticator. Any 2 of 3 channels must confirm sensitive actions. One compromised channel is never enough.

₿
Bitcoin Anchoring

Every vault gets an OpenTimestamps proof on the Bitcoin blockchain. A tamper-evident timestamp that survives Morrigan's own infrastructure.

🔑
Shamir's Secret Sharing

Split your vault key into N shares. Require M to reconstruct. Information-theoretically secure. Distribute across jurisdictions.

🌐
Open Source AGPLv3

Full source code on GitHub. Auditable, forkable, self-hostable. No proprietary dependencies. No black boxes.

🏠
Self-Hostable

Clone the repo and run your own instance. Your data on your infrastructure. Full INSTALL.md with Docker and manual instructions.

Roadmap

Where we're going.

Morrigan is actively developed. Every release brings the platform closer to a fully audited, post-quantum, community-governed foundation.

v1.0.0
Foundation Released
Multi-page site, dark design system, dual-channel authentication, blockchain anchoring via OpenTimestamps, beneficiary system.
v1.1.0
3-Channel Identity Current
2-of-3 identity system (email OTP, phone SMS, TOTP authenticator), TOTP UI, extended documentation, initial code audit preparation.
v1.2.0
PQC Migration Begins Planned
Full TOTP backend, Kyber-1024 post-quantum cryptography migration begins, improved vault versioning.
v1.3.0
Hardware Keys Planned
WebAuthn and hardware security key support (YubiKey, Passkeys), as a fourth identity channel option.
v1.4.0
Mobile Apps Planned
Native iOS and Android applications with offline vault access and biometric unlock.
v1.5.0
Multi-Party Vaults Planned
Collaborative wills, shared vault ownership, multi-party signing for sensitive actions.
v2.0.0
Independent Audit Planned
Independent cryptographic audit by a recognised security firm, public bug bounty programme, formal foundation registration.
