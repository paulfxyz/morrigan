All Features

Everything Morrigan can do.

Client-Side Encryption

All encryption happens in your browser. Your plaintext never leaves your device.

XChaCha20-Poly1305

256-bit symmetric cipher. Authenticated encryption. Resistant to nonce reuse.

Argon2id KDF

Memory-hard password hashing. Defeats GPU and ASIC dictionary attacks.

Shamir's Secret Sharing

Your vault key split into N shares. Any M shares reconstruct — not all needed.

Dead Man's Switch

Configurable intervals. Missed check-ins trigger the delivery chain.

Grace Period

A buffer after missed check-in — letting you override accidental triggers.

Beneficiary Roles

Primary, secondary, and witness roles. Layered access control.

Dual-Channel Auth

Email + phone required simultaneously. No single point of compromise.

Vault Versioning

Every edit creates a new version. Full history preserved and anchored.

Roadmap

Where we're going.

v1.0.0 Current

Vault creation, encrypted storage, dead man's switch, blockchain anchoring, dual-channel identity verification.
