How It Works

Every feature, deeply considered.

Four pillars that make Morrigan unlike any vault you've encountered.

Military-grade, client-side encryption

All encryption happens in your browser before any data touches our servers. We use XChaCha20-Poly1305 for authenticated encryption and Argon2id to derive keys from your passphrase — meaning your vault is mathematically inaccessible without your passphrase.

Even if our servers were seized, an attacker would find only meaningless ciphertext. We have designed the system so that we are incapable of reading your data.

  • ✓ XChaCha20-Poly1305 authenticated encryption
  • ✓ Argon2id (64MB, 3 iterations) key derivation
  • ✓ All encryption in the browser — zero server knowledge
Encryption layer
// Client-side only
const key = await argon2id({
  password: passphrase,
  memory: 65536,
  iterations: 3
});
 
const cipher = xchacha20poly1305(key);
const sealed = cipher.encrypt(data);
 
// sealed sent to server
// key never leaves browser

Automated delivery with human safeguards.

The dead man's switch monitors your activity and, if you stop responding to check-ins, begins the delivery process. You define the grace period, the confirmation method, and who receives what.

A customisable grace period — 30, 60, or 90 days — means false alarms are humanly impossible. Each check-in requires dual-channel confirmation (email + phone).

  • ✓ Configurable grace period (30–90 days)
  • ✓ Dual-channel check-in (email + SMS)
  • ✓ Per-beneficiary role and access control

Bitcoin as your permanent notary.

We hash your encrypted vault contents and anchor that hash to the Bitcoin blockchain via OpenTimestamps. This creates a timestamped, tamper-evident proof that your vault existed at a specific moment in time — without ever revealing your content.

Your beneficiaries receive a verifiable .ots proof file that can be checked by anyone, independently, without relying on Morrigan.

  • ✓ SHA-256 hash anchored in Bitcoin
  • ✓ OpenTimestamps Merkle tree proof
  • ✓ Independently verifiable .ots receipt

Granular control over who receives what.

Assign different roles to different beneficiaries. A family member can receive personal letters; a lawyer can receive the encrypted legal will; a trusted friend can hold a Shamir share for vault recovery.

Roles define access level, delivery timing, and decryption permissions — all configurable per beneficiary and per vault item.

  • ✓ Per-item beneficiary assignment
  • ✓ Role-based access (viewer, executor, key-holder)
  • ✓ Shamir shares distributed across trusted parties

All Features

Everything Morrigan can do.

Nine capabilities built with cryptographers, not marketers.

🔐

Client-Side Encryption

All encryption and decryption happens inside your browser. Zero server-side key access. Zero possibility of a provider-side breach exposing your plaintext.

⚡

XChaCha20-Poly1305

A 192-bit nonce stream cipher with authenticated encryption. Used by WireGuard and audited by the libsodium team. Resistant to timing attacks and nonce reuse errors.

🛡️

Argon2id KDF

Memory-hard key derivation requiring 64MB RAM and 3 iterations. Winner of the Password Hashing Competition. Renders GPU and ASIC brute-force attacks economically infeasible.

🧩

Shamir's Secret Sharing

Your vault key is split into N shares. Any M-of-N subset can reconstruct it. Distribute shares across trusted parties — no single point of failure, no single point of trust.

⏱️

Dead Man's Switch

Periodic check-ins confirm you're still active. Miss a check-in past your grace period and the delivery process begins. You remain in full control of the timing.

🕐

Grace Period

Set a 30, 60, or 90-day window after a missed check-in before delivery begins. Prevents accidental triggers during travel, illness, or digital detox.

👥

Beneficiary Roles

Assign executors, viewers, legal recipients, and Shamir key-holders. Each person receives exactly what you chose — no more, no less.

📱

Dual-Channel Auth

Critical actions require confirmation on both email and phone. An attacker who compromises one channel cannot export your vault, change beneficiaries, or trigger delivery.

📦

Vault Versioning

Every vault update creates a versioned snapshot. Restore any previous state. Your past intentions are preserved even as your present intentions change.
