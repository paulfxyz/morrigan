Legal terms

We can't recover your passphrase.

This is a core part of how Morrígan works — not a limitation we can work around.

Your passphrase is your key
Morrígan uses zero-knowledge encryption. Your vault is encrypted entirely in your browser with your passphrase — we never see it, store it, or touch it. There is no master key on our side.
If you've lost it, your vault is inaccessible
Without your passphrase, the encrypted data in your vault cannot be decrypted — by anyone. We are genuinely, technically incapable of helping you access it.
Your beneficiaries may still receive their capsule
If you set up beneficiaries, they will receive their designated capsule when your dead man's switch triggers — regardless of whether you can still log in. That part of Morrígan continues to work as designed.

If you think you may still remember parts of your passphrase, try variations carefully. If you set up a vault recently, check where you noted it down — a password manager, a physical document, or a secure note.
