Security

Built to be incapable of betraying you.

Zero-Knowledge

We can't read your data. By design.

Zero-knowledge means the server never receives your plaintext data. Your passphrase never leaves your device. Everything is encrypted in your browser before any data is transmitted to our infrastructure.

Encryption algorithm
AES-256-GCM
Authenticated encryption with additional data. Industry standard for symmetric encryption.
Key derivation
Argon2id
Memory-hard key derivation. Resistant to GPU and ASIC attacks on your passphrase.
Key storage
Client only
Your encryption key exists only in your browser's memory while you're logged in. Never transmitted.
Granular control

Every folder is its own vault.

Morrígan doesn't encrypt your data as one monolithic blob. Each folder you create can carry its own encryption key, its own key-derivation settings, and its own access protocol. Your medical records can require a hardware token. Your passwords can use a separate passphrase your family never knows. Your letters can sit behind a 2-of-3 Shamir share that only unlocks when two people agree.

Independent master keys
Each folder is encrypted under its own derived key. Compromising one folder's passphrase reveals nothing about any other.
Choose your cipher & protocol
Select AES-256-GCM, set Argon2id cost parameters, or layer on ChaCha20-Poly1305. Each folder is a fully configurable encryption context.
Granular access per folder
One folder can require a YubiKey. Another unlocks with Shamir shares split across three people. A third uses a passphrase only you and one beneficiary know.
No shared secrets across folders
Your vault-level passphrase never derives folder keys. Every folder is isolated — so you can share one folder with a lawyer without exposing anything else.

Argon2id·AES-256-GCM·Ed25519·TLS 1.3·WebAuthn·TOTP / RFC 6238·Cloudflare·AWS
Dead Man's Switch

Automatic delivery, only when the time comes.

Morrígan's dead man's switch is configurable, transparent, and designed with false positive prevention at every step.

1

Set your check-in interval

Choose daily, weekly, monthly, or a custom interval. Morrígan sends you a reminder before the deadline.

2

Receive reminders

Multiple reminders are sent across your configured channels. We escalate — first email, then emergency contacts.

3

Grace period begins

If no response, a configurable grace period starts. Additional verification attempts are made. This prevents accidental triggering.

4

Legacy is delivered

Your vault or specific sections are delivered to your designated beneficiaries according to your access rules.

What we store — and what we don't.

We store
  • ✓ Your email address
  • ✓ Encrypted vault ciphertext
  • ✓ Last check-in timestamp
  • ✓ Beneficiary email addresses (unencrypted)
  • ✓ Storage usage metrics
We never store
  • ✗ Your passphrase
  • ✗ Your encryption key
  • ✗ Decrypted vault contents
  • ✗ Browsing or usage behaviour
  • ✗ Any plaintext data at rest
