Security

Built to be incapable of betraying you.

Zero-Knowledge

We can't read your data. By design.

Zero-knowledge means the server never receives your plaintext data. Your passphrase never leaves your device. Everything is encrypted in your browser before any data is transmitted to our infrastructure.

Encryption algorithm
AES-256-GCM
Authenticated encryption with additional data. Industry standard for symmetric encryption.
Key derivation
Argon2id
Memory-hard key derivation. Resistant to GPU and ASIC attacks on your passphrase.
Key storage
Client only
Your encryption key exists only in your browser's memory while you're logged in. Never transmitted.
Dead Man's Switch

Automatic delivery, only when the time comes.

Morrígan's dead man's switch is configurable, transparent, and designed with false positive prevention at every step.

1. Set your check-in interval

Choose daily, weekly, monthly, or a custom interval. Morrígan sends you a reminder before the deadline.

2. Receive reminders

Multiple reminders are sent across your configured channels. We escalate — first email, then emergency contacts.

3. Grace period begins

If no response, a configurable grace period starts. Additional verification attempts are made. This prevents accidental triggering.

4. Legacy is delivered

Your vault or specific sections are delivered to your designated beneficiaries according to your access rules.

Open Source

The entire Morrígan codebase is publicly available on GitHub. Every cryptographic decision is documented. Community security reviews are welcome and encouraged.

Infrastructure

What we store — and what we don't.

We store
  • ✓ Your email address
  • ✓ Encrypted vault ciphertext
  • ✓ Last check-in timestamp
  • ✓ Beneficiary email addresses (unencrypted)
  • ✓ Storage usage metrics
We never store
  • ✗ Your passphrase
  • ✗ Your encryption key
  • ✗ Decrypted vault contents
  • ✗ Browsing or usage behaviour
  • ✗ Any plaintext data at rest
