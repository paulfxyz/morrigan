Once your vault is created, we'll guide you through adding at least two login options — a phone number for SMS codes, and an authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy, or any TOTP app). Both must stay active. This is not optional — it's how Morrígan keeps your vault sealed.

Important: Your passphrase is the only key to your vault. Morrígan uses zero-knowledge encryption — we can never recover your passphrase if you lose it. Store it somewhere safe, such as a physical document or a trusted password manager.

Privacy Policy

Data We Collect

We collect your email address, encrypted vault contents (ciphertext only), check-in activity, and payment metadata. We never collect your passphrase or plaintext data.

How We Use Your Data

Your email is used for account authentication, check-in reminders, and service communications. We do not sell your data to third parties.

Zero-Knowledge Principle

All vault content is encrypted client-side with AES-256-GCM before transmission. We store only ciphertext. Our servers cannot decrypt your data.

Data Retention

Active account data is retained for the duration of your subscription. On account deletion, all encrypted data is permanently purged within 30 days.

Third-Party Services

We use AWS and Cloudflare for infrastructure, Twilio for SMS notifications, and Persona for optional identity verification. Each provider is bound by data processing agreements.

Security

We use TLS 1.3 for data in transit, AES-256-GCM for data at rest, and Argon2id for key derivation. Our codebase is open-source and community-audited.
