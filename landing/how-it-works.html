How it Works

Four steps stand between now and forever.

1
Create your vault

Sign up with an email and a strong passphrase.

Your passphrase is used to derive your encryption key using Argon2id — a memory-hard algorithm designed to resist brute force. The passphrase never leaves your device.

Zero-knowledge from the first keystroke
Secured from signup

Granular control

Every folder is its own vault.

Morrígan doesn't encrypt your data as one monolithic blob. Each folder you create carries its own encryption key, its own key-derivation settings, and its own access protocol. Your medical records can require a hardware token. Your passwords can use a separate passphrase your family never knows. Your letters can sit behind a 2-of-3 Shamir share that only unlocks when two people agree.

Independent master keys
Each folder is encrypted under its own derived key. Compromising one folder's passphrase reveals nothing about any other.
Choose your cipher & protocol
Select AES-256-GCM, set Argon2id cost parameters, or layer on ChaCha20-Poly1305. Each folder is a fully configurable encryption context.
Granular access per folder
One folder can require a YubiKey. Another unlocks with Shamir shares split across three people. A third uses a passphrase only you and one beneficiary know.
No shared secrets across folders
Your vault-level passphrase never derives folder keys. Every folder is isolated — so you can share one folder with a lawyer without exposing anything else.
2
Add your secrets

Store passwords, files, messages, and accounts.

Upload any file type. Type notes and instructions. Store login credentials. Record voice messages. Everything is encrypted before upload using AES-256-GCM.

3
Set your beneficiaries

Choose who receives what — and when.

Designate one or more beneficiaries and assign them access to specific sections of your vault. Your partner can see finances. Your sibling gets the photos. You stay in full control.

Guardian and Legacy plans support unlimited beneficiaries

4
Configure the dead man's switch

Set your check-in interval and grace period.

Choose how often Morrígan should hear from you — weekly, monthly, or on a custom schedule. Set a grace period for how long to wait after a missed check-in before beginning delivery. You can update these settings at any time.

5
We deliver

Your legacy reaches the right people at the right time.

When the switch triggers, Morrígan delivers your vault sections to your beneficiaries. They receive a secure link, instructions you wrote, and access to their designated content. The process is documented in our open-source code — you can read exactly what happens.
