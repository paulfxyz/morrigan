Style Guide

Brand guidelines, design tokens, and visual standards for Morrígan.

Brand Overview

Morrígan is an open-source, non-profit digital legacy platform. Our brand is minimal, trustworthy, and grounded in clarity. Every design decision prioritises legibility, privacy, and permanence.

Logo

The Morrígan mark is a four-leaf clover (trèfle à quatre feuilles) — white on black — representing life, legacy, and the four cardinal directions. It is always presented as a white icon on a black rounded square.

  • Never alter the clover shape or proportions
  • Never place the logo on a coloured background (use white or black)
  • Minimum size: 24px height
  • Always pair with the wordmark Morrígan (with fada accent on the í)

Typography

Morrígan uses the system font stack for maximum legibility and performance:

  • Primary: Inter, SF Pro Display, -apple-system, sans-serif
  • Headings: 700 weight, tight letter-spacing (−1px to −0.3px)
  • Body: 400–500 weight, 1.6–1.8 line-height
  • Monospace: Used sparingly for code or technical references

Voice & Tone

  • Clear over clever — plain language, no jargon
  • Trustworthy — we handle sensitive information; every word reflects that responsibility
  • Understated — no exclamation points, no hype
  • Human — warmth and care for the person behind every legacy

Brand Name

Always write Morrígan with the fada accent on the í (Unicode U+00ED). Never write "Morrigan" without the accent in user-facing contexts. The name derives from Irish mythology — Morrígan, the goddess of fate and sovereignty.

Icons & Illustration

Use minimal, line-based icons at 24px or 20px. Icon colour should follow the accent system. Avoid decorative illustration; prefer functional, purposeful visuals.
